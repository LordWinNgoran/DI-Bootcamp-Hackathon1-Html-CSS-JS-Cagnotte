Gagnotte pour un Champion

This project is a game where the winner leaves with a prize depending on the number of points obtained. The biggest prize is a live concert where the user will be able to play the drums, the second is a drawing area, the third prize is a PDF book about microservices and the last prize is a video of Kevin Durant in the 2016 NBA finals. The player will earn his prize based on the number of points he gets. At the beginning of the game he will see a grid where the prizes will be displayed according to the points. This game starts with an identification form and after the identification phase. The player has a number of chances to play depending on the answer of a quick mental calculation.
